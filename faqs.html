What does शुभ विवाह mean?

शुभ विवाह (Shubh Vivah) directly translates to "Happy Marriage". But the meaning goes well beyond just that. It's the wish for an auspicious beginning of a lifetime together. Most Marathi wedding invitations have this blessing as invitations are the first "ceremony" of the full sequence of wedding celebrations.
