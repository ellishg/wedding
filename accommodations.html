Travel

Driving Instructions

The venue is located in the Morton Arboretum at

Thornhill Education Center, Glen Ellyn, IL 60137

﻿Google Maps Link﻿

Please navigate to the arboretum entrance on Ginkgo Way using Google Maps, which works better than Apple Maps. Tell the attendee our names and they will direct you to parking lot #21.

Visit Website

Hotels

The Morton Arboretum has several hotels nearby along Highway 88. This will get you very close to the venue as well as within 15 minutes from Downtown Naperville. We have blocks at 2 hotels but feel free to stay anywhere you prefer! Please note, hotel room blocks have separate deadlines to book in order to get discounted rates. Please reach out if you have any questions or want a room that doesn't seem available!

﻿

Courtyard Marriott

﻿Booking Link, Google Maps Link﻿

Less than 10 minute drive to the Morton Arboretum main gate! Also less than 10 minutes to Downtown Naperville, an area with great restaurants, bars, and lovely a River Walk. The Marriott has some nice communal gathering spaces, ideal for families traveling together to hang out.

﻿

Hyatt Regency

﻿Booking Link, Google Maps Link﻿

Basically right outside the Morton Arboretum and within 15 minute drive to Downtown Naperville, an area with great restaurants, bars, and a lovely River Walk. Additional charge for more than 2 occupants.﻿

Flights

If you need to fly, we recommend either O'Hare (ORD) or Midway (MDW). Both are about the same distance to the arboretum.
